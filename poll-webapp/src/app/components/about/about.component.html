Our Story

It was regular thursday Tom and I were in a dive bar deciding on which is the best NY brewery, after about 5 minutes of going back and forth we were at a point where we started taking opinions from random people in that bar. As time went by we took our phones out to ask other people opinions whom we have never met. So we went on couple websites to ask questions and hell broke loose. What we saw was mind boggling..These websites were asking these people to spend their hard earned money for asking simple questions.. We thought how hard could it be to come up with an application which is completely free to use for people who have genuine questions? On that evening in a dive bar Pollbag was born. Our mission is to arm people with superpowers of Pollbag so that you can ask questions to which you want unbaised opinions. And today, a year later we are here handing you the superpower in a form of simple and sophisticated application to ask as well as answer questions. Hope you enjoy it and get answers to whatever questions you have...
